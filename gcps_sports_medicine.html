Understanding DOK Levels

What are Depth of Knowledge levels?

DOK Level 1
Recall & Reproduce
Recall facts, definitions, and basic concepts. What is RICE? What does ACL stand for?
DOK Level 2
Skills & Concepts
Apply knowledge, compare, explain how/why. Why does cryotherapy reduce swelling?
DOK Level 3
Strategic Thinking
Analyze, evaluate, apply to scenarios. Which assessment finding BEST indicates a Grade III sprain?
